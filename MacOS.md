### [chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/)

1. 设置通过zsh从特定端口启动的别名

```sh
# 在.zshrc中添加别名
alias chrome="/Applications/Google\ Chrome.app/Contents/MacOS/Google\ Chrome --remote-debugging-port=9222"
```
